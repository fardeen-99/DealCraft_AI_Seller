## 🧠 The AI Psychology (The "Crack" Factor)

Traditional bots follow rigid rules. **DealCraft AI is different.** 

Our proprietary "Seller Persona" engine (powered by Gemini Pro) is trained on real-world negotiation psychology. 
- **Persuasion & Pressure**: If you're cold, logical, or even "abusive" (pushing the limits of a deal), the AI's internal state shifts.
- **The Breaking Point**: Like a real seller, the AI can become defensive, playful, or even "soft." If you frame your arguments correctly or apply consistent pressure, the AI's "will" to hold a high price can **crack**, allowing you to secure deals that a standard algorithm would never allow.
- **Dynamic Personas**: From "Ray," the street-smart veteran who's been around for 15 years, to others, each seller responds uniquely to your tone.
